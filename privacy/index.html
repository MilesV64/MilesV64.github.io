Practicing is a personal thing, and as such privacy is a top priority for Andante.

You are the only one with access to your practice sessions, journal entries, etc.

All of your personal data (your profiles, practice sessions, journal entries, notes, recordings) are stored locally on your iPhone, meaning your information is completely private and only accessible by you.

Error Logs

Apple collects error logs if you have chosen to allow it on your device. If allowed, Apple anonymously shares useful data to see what may have gone wrong.

Data Export

Andante Pro users have the option of exporting their practice data as a .csv file. This file contains your profile and practice data which can be manipulated or used in data analytics programs. The file is completely under your control and is only sent where you choose to send it when you export your data.
