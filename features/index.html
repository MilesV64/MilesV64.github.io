Practice

Log sessions

Keep track of your practicing by logging all your sessions. Name your sessions, write notes, and input your mood and focus levels. Easily refer back to a session with filters and search.

Manually add sessions

Forgot to record a session but still want to track your progress? Just add a session by providing the date, start time, and duration.

Audio recording

Andante has a built in audio recorder so you can record and playback your practicing at a moment's notice. Your recording will then be saved to the session so you can refer back to it later.

Metronome

Perfect your rhythm without ever having to leave the app.

Timer

A simple timer with a progress indicator for whenever you need a... timer.

Notes

Quickly jot down notes during your session or after you're done.

Motivate

Streaks

Motivate yourself to practice every day with the help of a streak counter.

Daily goals

Take streaks a step further by setting goals for how long you want to practice every day.

Charts and data

Visualize your progress with calendars and charts. See your practice time over the last 7 days, last month, or last year. See when you have the best mood by seeing trends between mood and time of day. And so much more!

Export data

Want to take it even further? Export your practice data as a .csv file so you can manipulate, analyze, and visualize your practice data however you want.

Optimize

Track your Mood

Your mood represents how you felt during your session.

Track your mood to notice when you feel great while you're practicing.

Track your Focus

Your focus represents how productive you were during a session, or how well you stayed on-task.

Identifying when you have the best focus can help you understand your own practicing.

Journal

Folders

Organize your thoughts by keeping different folders of journal entries.

Formatting

Journal entries have just enough rich text formatting to stay organized, while being as clean and out-of-the-way as possible so there's nothing in the way of your writing.

Clarity

Minimalist design

It's important that nothing gets in the way of your practicing. Andante is designed to be as clean and simple as possible while still providing robust features.

Multiple profiles

If you're practicing more than one thing, you can create multiple profiles to keep everything sepparate.

Privacy

Your data is private. Always.

Practicing is a personal thing. All of your practice and journal information is stored on your phone, and never leaves.
